Discover the Modules Included in Our BOOKEAZIE

User Registration and Authentication

Allows users to create an account in BOOKEAZIE, and login securely to access the system. It ensures the confidentiality and privacy of user data, preventing unauthorized access and protecting user information.

Appointment Scheduling

This module allows users to schedule appointments with service providers, view available time slots, and confirm appointments. It also handles conflicts and rescheduling.

Customer Management

This module manages customer data, including contact information, appointment history, and preferences. It helps service providers keep track of their customers and offer personalized service.

Calendar Management

Allows users to schedule appointments, view scheduled appointments and events on a calendar, and manage multiple calendars. It also provides options to set reminders, and notifications, and send invitations to attendees.

Reminder and Notification System

Sends automated reminders and notifications to both the appointment makers and clients, to ensure that all parties are aware of upcoming appointments. The reminders can be sent via email, text message, or even automated phone calls.

Staff Management

This module manages staff data, including their availability, skills, and schedules. It enables service providers to assign appointments to the right staff members based on their availability and expertise.

Reporting

This module provides detailed reports on appointments, staff performance, customer feedback, and other metrics. It helps service providers monitor their operations and make data-driven decisions.

Payment and Billing

This module handles payments and billing for appointments, including invoicing, payment processing, and refunds. It ensures that service providers get paid for their services and that customers have a seamless payment experience
